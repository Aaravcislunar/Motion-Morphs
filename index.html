Frequently asked questions

(Find answers to frequently asked questions about OneforEdits, our range of services, how we operate, and insights on maximizing the benefits of our agency services.)

Why should we choose your service?

Choose our service for high-quality video editing at a fraction of the cost of hiring a full-time senior-level editor, which typically exceeds ₹6,00,000 annually. With our flexible plans, you can choose to work on a project-basis as well as on affordable monthly plans . Additionally, we streamline freelancer management and quality assurance, ensuring a seamless experience for you.

How long does the video process take?

Our editors dive into your project the moment you provide the raw footage and the brief. With a commitment to quality, they focus on one project at a time, ensuring you're always a top priority. We strive for excellence with an optimized balance between quality and speed. You'll receive a delivery time based on your project's length, and we guarantee completion within that timeframe. If you have urgent needs, just let us know, and we'll accommodate.

How can I send big files to you?

There is no limit on size. You can send smaller files directly on Mail or you can submit files that are big in size through Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, or any other file transfer website that you prefer

How do you approach a project that requires custom effort?

If you have a complex project, don't worry. Reach out to us and we'll have a conversation tailored to your specific needs. Together, we'll address any challenges or concerns and work towards finding the perfect solution

How do revisions work if I’m not satisfied?

That’s highly unlikely, but in case you're not satisfied with the final product you have the right to a revision, you can always ask for one, and according to your requirements, changes will be made to the video.

How do I ensure the authenticity of this service?

Feel free to schedule a meeting with us at a time that is convenient for you. We are happy to address any and all of your concerns.
